# 🕐 Seasonal Safety Calendar — Month-by-Month Bug-Free Planning

> **Planning your trip?** Check this calendar to find the safest destinations for any month, and know exactly where to avoid.

---

## 🗓️ By Month

### January
- ✅ **Safe:** Dubai, Atacama Chile, Antarctica, Kona Hawaii, Hawaii
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, SE Asia (wet season begins in some areas)
- 💡 **Tip:** Winter in the N. hemisphere = dry season for all major deserts

### February
- ✅ **Safe:** Dubai, Atacama Chile, Hawaii, Kona, Sedona AZ, Las Vegas
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Bali (start of rainy season), Thailand
- 💡 **Tip:** Atacama is at its driest and most spectacular

### March
- ✅ **Safe:** Chile (coastal), Atacama, Sahara Morocco, Antarctica, Dubai
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Thailand, Costa Rica (start of wet season)
- 💡 **Tip:** Spring in the S. hemisphere = best time for Patagonia (winter = bug-free)

### April
- ✅ **Safe:** Hawaii, Kona, London, Seoul, Sedona, Las Vegas
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Bali, Mumbai
- 💡 **Tip:** Easter travel — deserts are perfect; tropics getting wet

### May
- ✅ **Safe:** Iceland, Scandinavia, Europe cities, Denver, Flagstaff
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Africa (start of malaria season), tropical Asia
- 💡 **Tip:** European cities are surprisingly low-bug due to cool, rainy climate

### June
- ✅ **Safe:** Iceland, Lofoten Norway, Svalbard, desert US, Atacama
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Thailand, Costa Rica, SO Asia
- ⚠️ **Note:** Arctic mosquito season begins mid-June — head further north for fewer bugs

### July
- ✅ **Safe:** Antarctica (peak season!), Kona Hawaii, Madeira, London, UK cities
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Bali, tropical S. America
- 💡 **Tip:** Antarctica is at its most accessible; book early!

### August
- ✅ **Safe:** Antarctica, Svalbard, Atacama, flagstaff, Denver
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Patagonia (sandflies begin), SE Asia
- 💡 **Tip:** Still safe in northern highlands and dry deserts

### September
- ✅ **Safe:** Canary Islands, Atacama Chile, Madeira, Dubai, Puebla Mexico
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, French Polynesia (start of wet season)
- 💡 **Tip:** Fall = dry season in Atacama; Canary Islands get warm and breezy

### October
- ✅ **Safe:** Atacama, Dubai, Iceland, Puebla Mexico, Sahara Morocco
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Bali, Thailand
- 💡 **Tip:** Desert destinations are golden across all continents

### November
- ✅ **Safe:** Dubai, Sahara Morocco, Antarctica, Atacama Chile, Honolulu
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, SE Asia (wet season in full swing)
- 💡 **Tip:** Start of Antarctic summer — Book 6+ months ahead

### December
- ✅ **Safe:** Antarctica, New Zealand, Chile (coastal), Dubai, Hawaii
- ⛔ **Avoid:** Amazon Basin, Africa (wet season), tropical Asia
- 💡 **Tip:** Southern hemisphere summer = best for NZ and Chile; Arctic best = bug-free deserts

## ⚠️ Seasonal Traps to Know

Even "bug-free" destinations have seasonal traps:

1. **Iceland** — Rare mosquitoes appeared in Oct 2025; verify current status before booking
2. **Patagonia (Argentina/Chile)** — June–August = safe; Nov–Mar = sandflies (culicoides) — DO NOT visit if phobic
3. **Hawaii** — Kona and Kohala coast = low bugs; Hilo side = much more mosquitoes
4. **Denver** — City = bug-free; Rocky Mountain National Park (higher elevations) = more mosquitoes in summer
5. **Galápagos** — Darwin Bay (Genovesa) has more insects than other islands; stick to Santa Cruz, San Cristóbal, Isabela

---

> 💡 **Key Insight:** Season matters MORE than location. A tropical destination in the dry season can be safer than a temperate one in the wet season. Always check current conditions!
