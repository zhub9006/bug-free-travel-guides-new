## 🎯 How to Contribute

### Tier 1: Share Your Experience
- [ ] Post a review of a bug-free destination you visited
- [ ] Share your travel tips in the [Discussion](https://github.com/zhub9006/bug-free-travel-guides-new/discussions)
- [ ] Star the repo to help it grow

### Tier 2: Research a Destination
- [ ] Pick a destination from the [Research Needed list](#-next-up--community-research-needed)
- [ ] Use the [Submission Template](guides/submission-template.md)
- [ ] Verify with 2+ independent sources
- [ ] Submit a PR

### Tier 3: Maintain & Improve
- [ ] Review PRs from other contributors
- [ ] Update outdated destination info
- [ ] Add new sources as they become available
- [ ] Help maintain the seasonal calendar

### Tier 4: Lead a Research Sprint
- [ ] Choose a region/theme for a monthly focus
- [ ] Recruit contributors
- [ ] Consolidate findings and merge
