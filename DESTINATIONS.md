# Bug-Free Travel Destination Guides

## 1. Iceland (Bug Level: ZERO)
No mosquitoes or ticks. Cold volcanic island.
- Best: Jun-Aug (10-15C)
- Activities: Golden Circle, Blue Lagoon, glacier hiking

## 2. Hawaii (Bug Level: LOW)
Island isolation = fewer insects.
- Best: Apr-Jun (24-29C)
- Tip: Stay leeward; choose A/C/screened stays

## 3. New Zealand (Bug Level: VERY LOW)
No sharks, no malaria, strict biosecurity.
- Best: Dec-Feb (20-30C)
- Tip: Sandflies only near Fiordland waterways

## 4. UAE (Bug Level: VERY LOW)
Desert climate, all air-conditioned.
- Best: Nov-Mar (20-30C)
- Tip: Carry a small repellent

## 5. Scotland Islands (Bug Level: LOW)
Orkney/Shetland virtually bug-free.
- Best: May-Sep
- Tip: Stick to wind-exposed coasts
